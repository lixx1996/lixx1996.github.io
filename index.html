Projects

Snapshot partially-coherent diffraction tomography based on light field microscopy


We build a light field microscope and achieve 3D phase retrieval within a single snapshot. This paper is submitted to Advanced Photonics and I am the joint first author.

Deep learning-based 3D Reconstruction for Light Field Microscopy


We use a U-net based method to improve the performance of 3D reconstrction for light field microscopy, providing results with much less artifact. I was invited to give an upgraded oral presentation on Biophotonics Congress, 2019.

3D Single Molecule Localization Microscopy via Deep Learning


We propose a deep-learning approach based on faster-RCNN to estimate 3D locations of multiple emitters over a large axial range with high accuracy. And we also develop a fiji-plugin of proposed method. I was invited to give an oral presentation on Biophotonics Congress, 2020.

3D Observation of Large-Scale Subcellular Dynamics in Vivo at the Millisecond Scale.


I participate in the construction of scanning light field imaging system and accelerating reconstruction algorithm. This paper is submitted to Cell.
